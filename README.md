## 🌸 About the Brand

**House of NYSA** is an affordable-luxury ethnic wear label for women who want well-fitting,
beautiful Indian outfits — without boutique price tags. We believe every woman deserves to
feel seen, stylish, and comfortable, which is why we design across a **truly inclusive size
range (S–3XL)** and offer personal, WhatsApp-first styling support.

- 👗 **What we make** — Ready-to-wear kurta sets, Anarkalis, salwar suits, sharara & coordinated dupatta sets
- 🎉 **For every moment** — Casual, festive, and formal occasions
- 📏 **Made to fit** — Inclusive sizing with a focus on styles that flatter every body
- 💬 **Personal touch** — Order & get styling help directly over WhatsApp
- 🚚 **Pan-India delivery** — Delivered to your door in 5–7 days
- 🧵 **Crafted with love in India**

> *"Wear Your Story" — fashion that's personal, empowering, and made for you.*
